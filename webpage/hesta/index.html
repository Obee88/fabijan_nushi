“We’re living in the golden age of technology, more specifically - smart technology. Do we?”

Hesta is an IoT application with a strong focus on user experience and interface design. Its intuitive navigation and user-friendly interface simplify the setup and management of IoT devices, making it accessible to users of all technological backgrounds. With Hesta, users can effortlessly connect and control a wide range of smart devices, personalize routines, and automate tasks. Privacy and security are prioritized through robust encryption protocols and strict access controls. Vesta's adaptability and scalability ensure seamless compatibility and integration with emerging devices and technologies, providing an evolving and improved IoT experience over time.

Hesta derives its name from the fusion of "Hestia" and "Vesta," representing the Roman and Greek goddesses of the home. This thoughtful selection of name epitomizes the users' dominion over their living spaces through the Hesta IoT application, accentuating the notion of effortlessly and efficiently managing diverse facets of home life.

A STRUCTURED APPROACH TO IOT UX RESEARCH
The growing demand for smart devices in the IoT landscape has fueled the expansion of smart homes worldwide. With an increasing number of households adopting smart home technology, there is a growing focus on user experience and interface design in IoT applications. The widespread adoption of smart devices, such as lighting, thermostats, security systems, and home entertainment systems, highlights the importance of intuitive UX and UI in enhancing the usability and accessibility of smart home solutions. As the global smart home device market continues to thrive, reaching an estimated value of $128.3 billion by 2028, the emphasis on user-centric design in IoT applications will play a crucial role in shaping the future of smart homes.
FORMS-BASED USER RESEARCH, DISCUSSION AND CONVERSATION
In the pursuit of creating a user-centric IoT product, we conducted a comprehensive three-day discovery phase that centered around user research. By engaging in forums, discussions, and conversations with users, as well as analyzing academic articles from the IoT industry, we were able to gain valuable insights into the expectations and needs of our target audience. Leveraging a combination of user research methods, we uncovered the nuanced details necessary to deliver a product that meets user expectations and addresses their specific requirements. Users emphasized three key points: frustration with device compatibility issues, difficulties in connecting multiple users and managing other devices, and the desire for more freedom in interface design to personalize the application according to individual needs rather than having a standard design for all users.
UI DESIGN FOR IOT HESTA APP
During the creation of the UI user interface for Hesta, we encountered various challenges that aimed to ensure seamless navigation and ease of use for our users in IoT applications. Our primary focus was to design an interface that simplifies the setup and management of IoT devices, making it effortless for users to connect and control their smart devices. Through extensive user research, discussions, and conversations, we were able to identify these challenges and work towards innovative solutions that prioritize user-centric design in IoT applications. Our goal was to create an interface that not only simplifies the IoT experience but also offers the flexibility and usability necessary to meet the evolving needs of our users.
Challenge we encountered was balancing the need for a personalized interface design while maintaining usability for all users. We understood the importance of allowing users to personalize the application according to their individual preferences and needs. However, we also recognized the significance of providing a standardized and user-friendly interface that caters to a diverse user base.
One of the key difficulties we faced was addressing the issue of device compatibility. We strived to create an interface that seamlessly integrates with a wide range of IoT devices, reducing the frustration users often experience when trying to connect and manage different devices from various manufacturers. Additionally, we aimed to address the challenge of managing multiple users and devices within the application, providing a streamlined and intuitive experience for households with multiple users.

The logo and color palette effectively emphasize both the brand name and the core concept of the product.
